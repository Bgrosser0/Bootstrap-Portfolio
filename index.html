About Me


Hello! My name is Ben Grosser and I am currently enrolled in the University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp. While I have just begun the program I have found the concepts and practices of coding engaging and rewarding. I hope to develop more skills and get a better grasp of the concepts as time goes on and turn them into a career.


When I am not coding I enjoy making music. I currently have a project called Iver Iverson that blends both electronic and acoustic elements into densely layered but accessable pop music.
